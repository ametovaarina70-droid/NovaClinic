Preparing for your surgical consultation

1 March 2025 · 3 min read

A surgical consultation is an important step in understanding your treatment options and planning the next steps of care.

Bring relevant medical information

Prepare a list of current medications, previous surgeries, allergies, and recent test results if available.

Prepare your questions

Write down any questions about the procedure, recovery time, risks, and alternatives so nothing is forgotten during the visit.

Understand next steps

Ask about pre-operative preparation, expected recovery, and follow-up care after surgery.

Being prepared helps ensure a productive consultation and allows you to make informed decisions.

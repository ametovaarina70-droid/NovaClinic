About the Surgeon

Dr. Hart is a board-certified surgeon with more than 15 years of practice. He specializes in minimally invasive procedures, laparoscopic operations, hernia repair and emergency abdominal surgery.

His approach focuses on safety, clear communication, and fast recovery with the lowest possible complication rates.

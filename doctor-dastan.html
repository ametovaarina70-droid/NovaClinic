Dr. Dastan Kubatbekov, MD

Vascular & Endovascular Surgery

About the Surgeon

Dr. Dastan Kubatbekov specializes in vascular and endovascular surgery, focusing on the diagnosis and surgical treatment of diseases affecting blood vessels and circulation. His clinical practice emphasizes minimally invasive endovascular techniques combined with classical vascular surgery when required.

He is committed to patient-centered care, clear communication, and evidence-based treatment planning to achieve long-term vascular health outcomes.

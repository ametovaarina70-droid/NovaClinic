About this service

General surgery addresses a wide range of conditions affecting the abdomen, digestive system, endocrine system, and soft tissues.

Our experienced surgical team focuses on safe, evidence-based procedures with an emphasis on minimally invasive techniques.

Procedures we perform

Hernia repair

Surgical repair of abdominal wall hernias using mesh reinforcement

Appendectomy

Emergency or planned removal of inflamed appendix

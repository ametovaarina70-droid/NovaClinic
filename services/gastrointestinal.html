About this service

Gastrointestinal surgery focuses on the diagnosis and surgical treatment of conditions affecting the digestive system, including the stomach, intestines, appendix, and related organs.

Our surgeons apply evidence-based techniques and modern technologies to ensure safe procedures and optimal patient outcomes.

Common procedures

Appendectomy

Removal of the appendix in cases of acute appendicitis

⏱ 30–60 min ✔ Short hospital stay

Bowel resection

Surgical removal of diseased segments of the intestine

⏱ 90–150 min ✔ Individual recovery plan

Hernia repair

Treatment of abdominal wall hernias affecting digestion

⏱ 45–90 min ✔ Fast recovery

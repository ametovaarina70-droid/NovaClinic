About this service

Oncologic surgery focuses on the surgical management of tumors and cancerous conditions, aiming to remove diseased tissue while preserving healthy structures whenever possible.

Our approach is multidisciplinary, combining surgery with oncology, radiology, and pathology to ensure precise diagnosis and treatment planning.

Common procedures

Tumor excision

Surgical removal of localized tumors with safety margins

⏱ 60–120 min ✔ Individual recovery plan

Lymph node dissection

Removal of affected lymph nodes for staging and treatment

⏱ 90–150 min ✔ Close post-operative monitoring

Reconstructive tumor surgery

Functional and aesthetic reconstruction following tumor removal

⏱ Variable ✔ Coordinated care

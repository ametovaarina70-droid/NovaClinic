About this service

Minimally invasive surgery (MIS) uses modern laparoscopic and endoscopic techniques to perform operations through small incisions instead of traditional open surgery.

These procedures significantly reduce tissue damage, postoperative pain, hospital stay, and recovery time.

Procedures we perform

Laparoscopic cholecystectomy

Removal of the gallbladder using laparoscopic instruments

⏱ 60–90 min ✔ 1–2 weeks recovery

Laparoscopic hernia repair

Minimally invasive repair of inguinal and abdominal hernias

⏱ 45–75 min ✔ 2–3 weeks recovery

Diagnostic laparoscopy

Exploratory procedure for diagnosis of abdominal conditions

⏱ 30–60 min ✔ Short recovery

Why choose MIS?

Minimally invasive surgery is designed to improve patient comfort while maintaining the highest surgical precision.

  • ✔ Smaller scars
  • ✔ Reduced pain
  • ✔ Lower complication risk
  • ✔ Faster return to daily life

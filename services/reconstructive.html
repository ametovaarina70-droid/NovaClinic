About this service

Reconstructive surgery aims to restore both appearance and functionality after injury, tumor removal, congenital conditions, or complex surgical interventions.

Our surgeons focus on personalized treatment plans that consider medical, functional, and aesthetic outcomes.

Common procedures

Post-traumatic reconstruction

Restoration of tissues and structures damaged by injury

⏱ Variable ✔ Staged recovery

Post-oncologic reconstruction

Reconstruction following tumor removal procedures

⏱ Variable ✔ Coordinated care

Soft tissue repair

Correction of soft tissue defects and functional impairment

Why choose reconstructive surgery?

Our reconstructive approach emphasizes precision, patient-centered planning, and long-term functionality.
